Call for Paper! SAWAE-ISAST 2025

5-7 August 2025 | Bandung, Indonesia

In 2025, the Southeast Asia Workshop on Aerospace Engineering (SAWAE) and the 11th International Seminar on Aerospace Science and Technology (ISAST) will be combined into a single prestigious event, bringing together researchers, academics, and industry experts in aerospace engineering and technology.

This joint conference aims to enhance collaboration, foster interdisciplinary discussions, and provide a comprehensive platform for sharing the latest advancements in the field. By merging SAWAE's focus on regional aerospace research with ISAST's broader international scope, the event will offer an enriched experience with diverse perspectives, cutting-edge insights, and expanded networking opportunities.

SAWAE at Glance

The Southeast Asia Workshop on Aerospace Engineering (SAWAE) is an annual international workshop organized by the Southeast Asia Network on Aerospace Engineering (SANAE). This event aims to explore the latest research and advancements in aerospace engineering and aviation, facilitate knowledge sharing and discussions among participants, and foster opportunities for future research collaborations and academic partnerships.

We are delighted to invite professors, researchers, scientists, and students to the 9th Southeast Asia Workshop on Aerospace Engineering (SAWAE), which will take place in Bandung, Indonesia, from 5th to 7th August 2025. This edition of SAWAE will feature a diverse range of keynote speeches and oral presentation sessions, covering various topics related to aerospace engineering and aviation.

ISAST at Glance

The 11th International Seminar on Aerospace Science and Technology (ISAST) 2025 is an international forum dedicated to advancing research, innovation, and collaboration in the field of aerospace science and technology. Bringing together experts, researchers, academics, and industry professionals from around the world, ISAST serves as a platform to exchange knowledge, discuss cutting-edge developments, and explore emerging trends shaping the future of aerospace engineering.

Scheduled to take place in Bandung Indonesia on 5-7 of August 2025, ISAST 2025 will feature keynote speeches from leading aerospace experts, technical paper presentations, and panel discussions covering a wide spectrum of topics, including aerodynamics, propulsion, avionics, space exploration, unmanned aerial systems, and sustainable aviation technologies. This seminar aims to foster interdisciplinary collaboration, strengthen industry-academia partnerships, and provide an avenue for researchers to showcase their latest findings.
